privacy policy.

Last Updated: March 24, 2026

1. introduction

At twyn, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your personal information when you use our website and AI wellness companion. Our core principle is simple: Your data is yours. We do not sell it.

2. data we collect

  • Usage Data: We use cookies to understand how visitors find us and interact with our site.
  • Health Data: With your permission, we access Apple Health data (steps, active energy) to provide insights.
  • Conversational Data: When you log meals or activity via chat, our AI processes this text to calculate nutritional estimates.

3. AI & processing

twyn uses artificial intelligence to interpret your natural language logs. This data is used solely to provide you with wellness metrics. We do not use your personal meal logs to train public AI models in a way that identifies you.

4. your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the EU/UK, you have the following rights:

  • The right to access the data we hold about you.
  • The right to request deletion of your data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

5. cookies

We use essential and analytical cookies. You can choose to decline non-essential cookies via the banner on our homepage. Declining cookies will not break your experience on twyn.
