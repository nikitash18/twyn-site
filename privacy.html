privacy policy.

last updated: March 24, 2026

our philosophy

your data belongs to you. twyn is designed to be a private companion, not a data broker. we don't sell your personal information, and we never will.

data we collect

we only collect information necessary to provide the wellness experience: your logged meals, activity data from apple health, and basic usage analytics to help us improve the app.

cookies

we use small text files (cookies) to understand how visitors interact with our site and to remember your preferences. you can decline these at any time via the banner on our home page.
