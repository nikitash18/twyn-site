building the brand.

week one was about more than just aesthetics; it was about defining the soul of twyn. We focused on establishing a visual identity and design system that balances personality with scalability. By launching our social presence early, we've begun building a community-led feedback loop before the first pixel of the app is even interacted with.

week 02

making it feel alive.

week two went deep on onboarding and UI. We mapped the full user flow to minimise cognitive load, ensuring the path to the "Aha!" moment feels effortless. We refined the micro-animations that make interactions feel considered and got Apple Sign-in working end-to-end to remove entry friction. The small stuff - the transitions, the timing, the moments between taps - is what defines the mental model and separates an app people tolerate from one they truly love using.
